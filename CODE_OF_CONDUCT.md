# Code of Conduct

We value the participation of every contributor and want this project to be a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior, and the steps for reporting unacceptable behavior.

## Our Standards

- Be welcoming and friendly. Treat others with respect and empathy.
- Be patient and assume good intent when communicating.
- Be collaborative: give and accept constructive feedback graciously.
- Be mindful of language and time zones, and avoid belittling or exclusionary remarks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are examples of unacceptable behavior and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Derogatory comments, slurs, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, sustained insults, or threats.
- Posting sexually explicit content or otherwise offensive material.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. If a contributor engages in unacceptable behavior, maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including: warning the offender, temporary or permanent removal from the project, or notifying the contributor's employer or relevant authorities if appropriate.

## Reporting Guidelines

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it as soon as possible. You can report incidents by opening a private issue (if available) or emailing the maintainers at: maintainers@yourproject.org

When reporting, include:

- A description of the incident (what happened, when, and where).
- Any relevant screenshots, links, or logs.
- The names or handles of any involved parties, if known.

Reports will be handled discreetly and promptly. Maintainers will acknowledge receipt within a reasonable timeframe and follow up to explain next steps and outcomes.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issue trackers, mailing lists, chat rooms, social media, and in-person events) when the codebase or project name is represented.
